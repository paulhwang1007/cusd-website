Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to join CUSD?

No prior experience is required for most teams! We welcome students from all backgrounds and skill levels. What's most important is your passion for sustainability and willingness to learn.

Can I join multiple teams?

While we recommend focusing on one team to maximize your impact and learning, some members do participate in multiple teams. Discuss this possibility with team leads during the application process.

What makes a strong application?

Strong applications demonstrate genuine interest in sustainability, clear communication, and specific reasons for wanting to join particular teams. Be authentic and show how your unique perspective can contribute to our mission.

Have More Questions?

If you have any questions about the application process or want to learn more about CUSD, please feel free to reach out to us on any of our socials!
